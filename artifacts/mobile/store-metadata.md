## Full Description (max ~4000 chars)

The Trading Mentor is the all-in-one mobile companion for traders learning and applying Inner Circle Trader (ICT) methodology. Whether you're studying market structure, Fair Value Gaps, or Optimal Trade Entry, this app keeps every concept, checklist, and journal entry in your pocket.

**Learn**
- Academy module with step-by-step ICT concept guides
- Visual chart examples for FVGs, MSS, OTE, Kill Zones, and more
- Structured learning paths from beginner to advanced

**Plan**
- Pre-session trade planning with structured templates
- Kill Zone scheduler aligned to market sessions (London, New York, Asia)
- Bias confirmation checklists before every trade

**Track & Journal**
- Log every trade with entry/exit, setup type, and screenshots
- Track win rate, RR ratio, and daily P&L automatically
- Annotate trades with notes and post-trade reviews

**Analyze**
- Analytics dashboard showing performance by setup type, session, and day
- Streak tracking and consistency scoring
- Identify your edge with data-driven insights

**Community**
- Share trade ideas and setups with fellow ICT traders
- Discuss concepts and get feedback from the community

**Subscription**
- Free tier with access to core academy content and limited journaling
- Pro subscription unlocks unlimited journaling, analytics, and community features
