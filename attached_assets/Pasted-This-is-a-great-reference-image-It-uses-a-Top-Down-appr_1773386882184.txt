📈 NQ Futures: ICT Trading Plan
The Tools (The Indicators)
 * MSS (Market Structure Shift): Our signal that the trend has changed.
 * FVG (Fair Value Gap): Our entry zone.
 * Liquidity (Internal/External): Our targets (Old Highs/Lows).
 * Premium vs. Discount (Fibonacci): We only buy in Discount and sell in Premium.
 * Kill Zones: London (2-5 AM EST) and NY Silver Bullet (10-11 AM EST).
Timeframe Alignment
 * Higher Timeframe (HTF): Daily & 1-Hour.
   * Purpose: Used to find the "Draw on Liquidity" (where is price going?).
 * Lower Timeframe (LTF): 15-Minute & 5-Minute.
   * Purpose: Used to find the MSS and the FVG entry.
Entry Criteria (Conservative)
 * Bias Check: Is the 1-Hour chart Bullish or Bearish?
 * The Sweep: Wait for price to take out a 15-minute High or Low.
 * The Shift: Wait for a 5-minute Market Structure Shift (MSS) with "Displacement" (a fast move).
 * The Gap: Identify the Fair Value Gap (FVG) left behind.
 * The Fib: Use the Fibonacci tool. Ensure the entry is in the Discount (for buys) or Premium (for sells).
 * The Trigger: Place a Limit Order at the start of the FVG.
Aggressive Entry (The Silver Bullet)
 * Time Check: Must be between 10:00 AM and 11:00 AM EST.
 * Identify POI: Price must be heading toward a clear High or Low.
 * The Gap: Enter at the first 1-minute FVG that forms after a liquidity grab.
 * Risk: Max 1% risk per trade.
Exit Criteria (Take Profit & Stop Loss)
 * Stop Loss (SL): Placed at the high/low of the candle that created the MSS.
 * Take Profit 1 (TP1): The next "Internal" High or Low (1:1 or 1:2 ratio).
 * Take Profit 2 (TP2): The "External" Liquidity (The main target).
 * Trailing: Move Stop Loss to "Breakeven" once TP1 is hit.
Prop Firm Survival Rules
 * Max Daily Loss: 2% (If hit, app locks for 24 hours).
 * Max Weekly Loss: 4%.
 * News Rule: No trading 5 minutes before or after "Red Folder" news.
Summary Key Takeaways
 * Top-Down: Always start with the Daily chart. If the Daily is going down, don't try to buy on the 1-minute.
 * Patience: If the market doesn't hit your FVG, there is no trade.
 * Discipline: Following this plan is how you get funded. Breaking it is how you stay a "student."
