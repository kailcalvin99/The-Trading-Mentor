Step-by-Step Reason for This Prompt
 * WebP Images: These are much smaller than PNG or JPG files, making the "engine" of your site lighter.

 * Width/Height: If a box doesn't have a size set, the page "jumps" when the image finally appears. This fixes that.
 * Minifying: This removes all the extra spaces and comments in your code that humans need but computers don't.
